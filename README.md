## 📝 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes industry survey feedback from **630 data professionals** across global markets (including the United States, India, United Kingdom, and Canada). The goal of this project is to uncover workforce demographics, salary distributions across job titles, programming preferences, and overall career satisfaction levels to help tech organizations optimize recruitment and retention strategies.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
* **🐍 Python Dominates the Field:** Python is overwhelmingly the favorite programming language among the surveyed tech professionals, significantly outperforming R, C/C++, JavaScript, and Java. 
* **💼 Salary Breakdown by Title:** Data Engineers hold the highest average salary segment among the core roles, closely followed by Data Analysts and Data Scientists.
* **⚖️ Job vs. Salary Satisfaction:** On a 10-point scale, data professionals rated their general work-life satisfaction at a moderate **5.74**, while salary satisfaction sits lower at **4.27**, indicating that compensation packages remain a major point of workforce tension.
* **👥 Demographics:** The global data workforce leans young, with an average survey taker age of **29.87 years**.
